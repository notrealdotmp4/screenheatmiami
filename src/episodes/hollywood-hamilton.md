Sean "Hollywood" Hamilton is a National Radio Hall of Fame inductee with over four decades of experience, hosting top-rated shows at Z100, KTU in New York, and KIIS-FM in Los Angeles. Known for his engaging roadcasting style, Hamilton has built a loyal fan base and earned widespread respect in the music industry. His radio feature War Of The Roses is one of the most popular in radio history and around the country.


His radio journey began at 16 when he built an illegal pirate radio station in his parents' basement, sparking his lifelong passion for broadcasting. He admits, “I knew I wanted to pursue a career in this business when I caught the bug back then illegally, speaking to six people on a clear day.


In addition to his radio career, Hamilton showcased his versatility by playing a key role in the Amazon Prime series
Gravesend, expanding his influence beyond the airwaves.
