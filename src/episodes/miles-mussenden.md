Miles Mussenden is a British-born American actor, filmmaker, producer, and director known for his commanding screen presence and emotionally grounded performances across film and television. With over a decade of professional experience, he has built a reputation as a versatile character actor capable of delivering both intensity and nuance.

Miles is widely recognized for his series regular role as Otis Johnson in the Marvel television series Marvel's Cloak & Dagger, as well as standout performances in acclaimed productions including The Good Lord Bird, Bloodline, The Walking Dead, Stranger Things, and The Blacklist. He also brought a memorable edge to the DC Universe as the super-villain Frenzy in Doom Patrol. He has continued to build momentum with recurring roles in Tulsa King, Swagger, and Based on a True Story. Upcoming projects include a recurring role in the Apple TV+ series Cape Fear and a lead role in the feature film Croatoan.

Expanding beyond acting, Miles has established himself as a filmmaker with his directorial debut, Homage, an urban crime drama exploring loyalty, redemption, and the lasting consequences of one's past.
